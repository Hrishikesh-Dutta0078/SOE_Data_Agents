Key Design Decisions

  • Headline insight — the LLM-generated first takeaway becomes a bold title. Gives the card instant scanability.
  • Inline mini-chart — a small, contextual chart sits inside the narrative. Clicking "Full chart" expands to full interactive recharts view.
  • Classification badges removed — SQL_QUERY / MODERATE are internal pipeline info. Executives don't need them. Moved to the Entities pill for power users.
  • Entity card removed from default view — accessible via "Entities" pill for those who want it.
  • Confidence as a dot + text — green dot + "High confidence" is subtler and more glanceable than a colored badge.
  • Progressive disclosure pills — table, full chart, SQL, entities, copy. Primary actions (table, chart) are accent-colored; secondary (SQL, entities) are muted.
